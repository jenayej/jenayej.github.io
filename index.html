Hi there! Jenaye Johnson (she/they) is a New York-based journalist who merges her background in data-driven science reportage with colorful visuals and multimedia.

She currently covers neuroscience, biology, the environment, and tech (biotech, clean tech, and climate tech). She has also produced an audio series for her Substack, *glue&glia*. Previously, they worked for a translational epilepsy center and served as a science communicator for the NASA Glenn Research Center. Their work has been featured in *Scienceline*.

Jenaye holds a master’s degree in Science, Health, and Environmental Reporting from New York University. She also provides writing, editing, and fact-checking services.
